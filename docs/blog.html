Soul Stock 2025

August 2025

Soul Stock 2025 was an incredible experience that brought people together through music, creativity, and community. The event featured local bands, artists, and food vendors, all set in an atmosphere full of energy and connection. Capturing moments from the day reminded me why I love working with live events—every shot tells a story of joy, spontaneity, and shared experience.
